**Business Model (Post-Validation):**

- Decision pending validation of internal use across 10+ real sales calls

## Problem Statement

**For Pagepro (Internal):**
1. Manual website audits take 8-16 hours per project
2. Inconsistent discovery process across different team members
3. Difficult to accurately scope migration complexity early
4. Hard to differentiate from competing agencies in sales process
5. Risk of underestimating effort and losing margin

**For Clients (Digital Native SMBs):**
1. Don't know what content/pages they actually have
2. Fear of SEO loss during migration (biggest blocker)
3. Uncertainty about migration timeline and cost
4. Can't articulate current website structure to vendors
5. "We should probably migrate everything" mindset (scope bloat)

### Internal Operators

**"Executive Sponsor Eva"** -- CEO/COO. Focused on ROI, timeline, risk. Needs clear business case with numbers.

2. **Competitor Analysis** -- $1,000 add-on later. Not MVP.
3. **White-Label** -- Not for 2026. Agencies are competitors.

## Risks & Mitigation

1. **Crawler blocked by anti-bot** -- Firecrawl handles most cases. Fallback: client provides sitemap/admin access.
2. **AI inaccuracy** -- Track accuracy, iterate prompts, budget 90min human review per audit.
3. **Low conversion** -- Validate with beta clients. Include walkthrough call. Money-back guarantee option.
4. **AI cost spikes** -- Monitor per-audit costs (target <$15). Use Haiku for classification, cache results.
